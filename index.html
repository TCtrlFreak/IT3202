History of the World Wide Web

The development and evolution of the World Wide Web has been a massive leap in the advancement and development of society. It has revolutionized the way that information is collected, stored, and spread amongst people of different nations and cultures. Over the course of 30+ years, the web has evolved from simple internet applications to modern mobile web applications with advanced design and architectures, with one of the most significant web technologies being HTML.
